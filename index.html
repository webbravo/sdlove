Our Story

How we Met

“Once in a while, right in the middle of an ordinary life, Love gives us a fairytale” I’d like to think that he was a gift the Universe gave to me after an unexpected pandemic. We met for the first time in a parking lot just outside a mall in Warri, We got talking and officially started dating 3 months after. Since then, he has been all the things I could ever ask for, my best friend, my gist partner and sometimes my quarrel buddy. He has taught me a lot about loving someone and wanting to be the best for them, I feel loved and I know he does too.

The Proposal

In August 14th 2022, I made a random trip to Asaba from Lagos, It was supposed to be a normal trip where we caught up and spend as much time together because I moved to Lagos for a job while he remained in Asaba, so we operated a long distance relationship' Little did I know he has something special waiting for me. He popped the sweet question, and of course I said yes with all my heart. If I was to pick who I’d do life with, I’d pick him over and over again, He will always be my baby and I can't wait to do forever with him.
